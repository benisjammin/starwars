Welcome

This is a derivative/distant-cousin project of Exoplanet Catalogue â€” and the Exoplanet iPhone app. This is to be an accessible, usable resource for the visualisation of the Star Wars galaxy, it’s star systems, planets, and other significant (and insignificant) artifacts.

This is a library of all systems and systems data, both canon and legends, in the Star Wars universe.

Objectives

  • Cataloging of all (known) systems. This includes:
    • Systems
      • Planets
      • Cities
    • Astronomical Phenomenon
  • Building a webapp to visualise, in simulated 3-d, the data.

Early focus is on compiling a library of system data (positions, orbits, mass(?), tep(?), transit time, radius, et cetera) to be used in the visual map.

The map itself will be a simulated three-dimensional projection of the galaxy. It may be best to use HTML5 to render the data.
